Unit 3: A Digital Portfolio

In this unit, I was required to design and develop a digital website portfolio to display the collection of work produced during my BTEC Level 2 IT course. The purpose of this was to demonstrate my skills and achievements, on a digital portfolio to potential employers or clients. This unit teaches how a portfolio is created, by looking closely at the planning and development life cycle of the portfolio project

A - design a digital portfolio
B - create and test a digital portfolio
C - review the digital portfolio
