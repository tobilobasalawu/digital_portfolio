Unit 13: Website Development

In this unit, have been asked to investigate the features and uses of websites by exploring what they are and how their integrated components and applications interact with each other. I learnt how to design, develop and test a website for a brief. Once it was completed I reviwed it, having obtained feedback from others.

A - understand the uses and features of websites
B - design a website
C - develop and test a website
D - review the finished website

Resources (Assignment & Tasks)
